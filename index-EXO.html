Let's introduce myself !

Hello dear friends! My name is Margaux. I've never thought I could study graphic design, but here I am, a 23 woman actually studying in ECV Bordeaux (School Of Visual Communication). Applying for a mastere education, I took an option which is to learn Design & Strategy in Shanghai for 4 months with the ESSCA (School of commercials sciences of Angers). I'm supposed to get there in January, and then I continue the final year in ECV Paris. Ok for my introduction, but what about my work ? All my project have been made during my studies, from my first diploma to my futur second diploma (2018). If I had put them here, this is because I think people that watch my Behance Profile can see who I am, what I can do and what I love to do. You may see a progress between the first ones I put online and those I recently put. About what I love in graphic Design. Curious and open-minded, I have quickly understood that each work can offer me something and that I can leave something from me in all of them. I like doing branding, playing with typefaces, choosing colors, create patterns, etc. Actually, I like creating the background (concept, philosophy) as much as the form. Enjoy my profile ;) see ya!
